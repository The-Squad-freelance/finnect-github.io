Focus on your field

Search for a freelancer who specializes in your industry. Need a professional who knows e-commerce? Real estate? Tech? Fine-tune your search to find a consultant who gets you and your business

Identify your problem

Get a handle on what you need help with or what you’re trying to achieve. Once you have that down, you’ll find it much easier to communicate with consultants, pick the one for you, and set expectations.

Browse the background

Once you’ve picked a few consultants that might be relevant, do a deeper dive into their experience and look over reviews to get firsthand customer feedback and choose just the right freelancer for your needs.

Start the conversation

Once you’ve made your selection, reach out to your consultant by requesting a video call to share expectations, get to know each other, and start working together.
